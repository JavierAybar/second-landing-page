This is Batman

The best thing about Batman is that he is human. A mere human just like us. What made him special is his courage, determination and not to forget his dedication. Born from the tragic death of his parents, Batman is just an ordinary man with extraordinary dedication to fight criminals; to perish all the bad guys, so that no more children could suffer like he did.
